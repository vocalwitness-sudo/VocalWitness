Safety & Community Guidelines

VocalWitness is a friendly public square where people can share their truths and experiences in a safe, respectful environment. We want everyone to feel heard and protected.

🌟 Speak Your Truth Respectfully

Share what you genuinely witnessed. We encourage honesty and constructive expression.

🛡️ Protect Privacy

Do not share PII (Personally Identifiable Information) that could put you or others at risk. Your safety is our priority.

🤝 Foster Dialogue

This is a space for truth-seeking, not for harassment or hate. We discourage content that promotes violence or discrimination.

🔍 Gentle Moderation

We use automated systems to encourage respectful language. Our goal is to ensure your message reaches as many people as possible safely.
