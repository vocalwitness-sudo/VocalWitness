Safety & Community Guidelines

VocalWitness is a friendly public square where people can share their truths, experiences, and testimonies in a safe and respectful way. We want everyone to feel heard and protected.

🌟 Speak Your Truth Respectfully

Share what you genuinely witnessed or experienced. We encourage honesty, but we ask you to express yourself in a way that others can receive and understand.

🛡️ Protect Privacy & Safety

Do not share personal information that could put yourself or others at risk. We use gentle moderation and verification tools to help create a safer space.

🤝 Build Understanding Together

This is a space for dialogue and truth-seeking, not for attacks or hate. Passionate testimonies are welcome, but we gently discourage content that promotes violence, discrimination, or harm.

🔍 Our Gentle Moderation

We use light automated checks to encourage respectful language. If something feels too strong, you may see a friendly suggestion to soften it. Our goal is not to silence voices, but to help your message reach more hearts.

Remember: VocalWitness is an un-manipulated distribution medium. Users are responsible for what they share. Always act with integrity and care.
