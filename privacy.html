Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. The Nature of VocalWitness

VocalWitness is a decentralized public square designed for truth and evidence. By design, content posted to the Citizen Square or the Forensic Ledger is public, permanent, and immutable. Please do not share sensitive personal information (such as private addresses or government IDs) in public testimonies.

2. Data We Collect

  • Identity Data: Email and profile identifiers used for authentication.
  • Content Data: The text, photos, and voice recordings you upload.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses and device info for security, bot prevention, and service integrity.

3. Data Usage & ZK-Proofs

We utilize Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs) to verify user legitimacy without exposing real-world identities. Data is used solely to maintain the integrity of the ledger and ensure the platform remains a safe, verifiable space.

4. Your Rights

You may request the deletion of your account at any time. However, due to the decentralized/ledger nature of the platform, content already published to the public square may remain visible as part of the immutable record.
