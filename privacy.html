Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

1. Our Commitment

VocalWitness is built on the principle that truth should not require sacrificing privacy.

We use Zero-Knowledge (ZK) cryptography so you can participate without exposing your real identity. Your testimony belongs to you.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • National ID, passport, or government documents
  • Biometric data
  • Precise real-time location data
  • Personal contacts or social graphs

We strongly advise against posting sensitive personal information in public testimonies.

3. What We Collect & Why

Content You Share

Text, photos, and voice recordings you voluntarily publish. This is public by design.

Technical Data

Anonymous usage data for security, spam prevention, and platform stability.

4. Zero-Knowledge Verification

We use ZK proofs to verify that users are real humans without collecting identifiable information. This helps maintain a high-trust environment while protecting anonymity.

5. Your Rights & Immutable Content

You can delete your account at any time. However, content already published to the public ledger is part of the permanent record and cannot be deleted — just like public speech in the real world.

This policy is designed to be transparent and to protect both users and the integrity of the platform. We do not sell data. We do not work with surveillance entities.
