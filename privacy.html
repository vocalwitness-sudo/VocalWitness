Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At VocalWitness, we believe your voice matters — and so does your privacy. We are committed to being transparent and responsible with your data.

1. Our Promise

VocalWitness is a platform for sharing testimonies and evidence. We collect only the information necessary to provide a safe and functional service.

2. Information We Collect

  • 👤
    Account information (email, display name, profile photo) when you sign up or log in.
  • 📝
    Testimonies, photos, voice recordings, and other content you choose to publish.
  • 🔐
    Technical data (device info, IP address) for security, moderation, and platform improvement.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the platform and enable you to share testimonies
  • To provide verification and forensic features (hashing, ZK proofs)
  • To maintain safety through gentle moderation
  • To improve the service and respond to support requests

4. Data Storage & Public Content

Your published testimonies are designed to be public and permanent once shared — this is the nature of a public square. We use secure Firebase infrastructure (Google Cloud) to store data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal account data
  • Request deletion of your account (this will not remove public testimonies already shared)
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Note: Because testimonies are public by design, we cannot guarantee removal of content once it has been published and viewed by others.
