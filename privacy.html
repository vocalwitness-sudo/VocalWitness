Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

VocalWitness is a decentralized platform for sharing testimonies. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. What Information We Collect

  • Email address and profile information when you sign in with Google
  • Testimonies, photos, and voice recordings you publish
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type) for security

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Operate and improve the platform
  • Display your public testimonies
  • Provide verification features

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud). Testimonies are designed to be public and permanent once published.

5. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting us.
