About VocalWitness

Your Voice. Your Truth. Shared with Integrity.

Our Mission

In an era of deepfakes, misinformation, and centralized control, VocalWitness restores the public square. We believe truth is not dictated by algorithms or gatekeepers — it is evidenced, witnessed, and verified by the people.

We empower every citizen to share raw testimonies, forensic media, and lived experiences with cryptographic integrity tools, while protecting privacy and resisting manipulation.

🔬

Forensic Integrity

Photos and voice recordings receive cryptographic hashing. Advanced verification (including Zero-Knowledge proofs in development) helps establish authenticity without revealing your identity.

🛡️

Privacy First

Your identity remains protected. We use phone verification, reputation systems, and privacy-preserving technology so you can speak boldly while staying safe.

🌍

Community Stewardship

No single entity controls the narrative. Trust is earned through honest participation. Verified Witnesses and reputation mechanics reward integrity.

🤝

Community Supported

Fully funded by voluntary sponsorships and willing donations from people who believe in a healthier information ecosystem. No paywalls. No premium features locked behind payments.

How We Build Trust

1. Citizen Talk → True Witness

Start as a Citizen and earn higher trust levels through phone verification and consistent honest contributions. True Witness mode offers the strongest cryptographic guarantees.

2. Forensic Ledger

All media is timestamped and hashed. The Evidence Ledger preserves permanent, verifiable records of what was shared.

3. Un-manipulated Distribution

We function strictly as a neutral public square and distribution medium. We do not edit, boost, or suppress content except to enforce basic safety and legal requirements.
