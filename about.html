About VocalWitness

We are building a safe and friendly public square where ordinary people can share their truths, testimonies, and evidence — with integrity and respect.

🌍 Our Mission

In a world full of noise and doubt, VocalWitness gives every citizen a voice. We provide tools for verified, real-time testimonies so that truth can be heard clearly and preserved honestly.

🛡️ What Makes Us Different

  • 🔬
    Forensic Tools
    Voice recordings, photos, and testimonies come with integrity proofs (hashing + ZK technology).
  • 🕊️
    Gentle & Safe Space
    We use light moderation to encourage respectful dialogue while protecting the community.
  • 🏟️
    Live Arena
    Real-time verified voices sharing testimonies live.

Our Promise

"Your Voice. Your Truth. Shared with Integrity."

VocalWitness is an un-manipulated decentralized distribution medium. We do not verify the absolute truth of every testimony — that responsibility lies with the community and those who witness it. We simply provide the tools and the space for voices to be heard.
