About VocalWitness

Restoring trust through cryptographic truth and community stewardship.

Our Mission

In an era of deepfakes, misinformation, and centralized censorship, VocalWitness was built to provide a resilient, immutable public square. We believe that truth is not something to be dictated by platforms—it is something to be evidenced, witnessed, and verified by the people.

We empower every citizen to become a witness, providing the tools to secure testimonies with forensic integrity and zero-knowledge privacy.

🛡️

Forensic Integrity

Every testimony is shielded by cryptographic proof, ensuring that evidence remains untampered and verifiable by the community.

⚖️

Community Stewardship

No single moderator controls the narrative. Reputation is earned through honest, verified contributions to the square.

How We Build Trust

VocalWitness operates on a meritocratic model. By combining decentralized storage with ZK-proof verification, we ensure that while your identity remains private, your testimony gains the weight of cryptographic certainty.
